  • What is a Grievance

    • A grievance is defined as "an official statement of a complaint over something believed to be wrong or unfair."

    A grievance can be raised by any stakeholder of the Institute such as a student, faculty or a parent

  • What is GMRS

    • Grievance Monitoring and Redressal System (GMRS) is the online portal of Sir MVIT for registering and disposing of grievances.

    The portal facilitates the students, faculty and parents to lodge and track their grievances

    The portal facilitates the various committees constituted at Sir MVIT to track and address the grievances

    GMRS fulfills the AICTE directive of online Grievance redressal mechanism

  • What are the Valid and invalid types of grievances

    • Valid grievances:

    • Ragging related issues
    • Harassment in the campus
    • Transport problems
    • Infrastructure related problems
    • Women related issues

    Invalid grievances:

    • Student infighting in the Campus
    • Loss of Mobiles, Laptops etc.
    • Temporary loss of Wi-fi connectivity
